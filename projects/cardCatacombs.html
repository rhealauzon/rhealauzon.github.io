Card Catacombs

Card Catacombs is an online multiplayer dungeon crawler for 2-4 players. One player, selected to be the Dungeon Master, is tasked with placing traps throughout each level, making them as challenging as possible. The other players, the Heroes, must journey through each level, fighting to survive to the end of the campaign.

Card Catacombs was created by myself and Christofer Klassen in C# using XNA and a custom reliable UDP networking protocol system.
